Our Menu

chicken pakora

Chicken Pakora is a crispy and spicy deep-fried snack made by coating small chicken pieces in a seasoned gram-flour batter. It is a popular Indian appetizer enjoyed with chutney.

ice cream

Ice cream is a sweet, frozen dessert made from milk, cream, sugar, and flavors. It is enjoyed cold and comes in many varieties like vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry.

chili chicken

Chili Chicken is a spicy and flavorful Indo-Chinese dish made with fried chicken pieces tossed in a tangy sauce of chilies, soy sauce, and vegetables..

nolen gurer rosogolla

Nolen Gurer Rosogolla is a soft, spongy Bengali sweet soaked in fragrant date palm jaggery syrup, loved for its warm caramel flavour and seasonal richness..

tomato chutney

Tomato chutney is a tangy, spicy, and flavorful side dish made mainly from ripe tomatoes.
